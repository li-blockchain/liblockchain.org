What is a Cryptocurrency

Lets break it down

Crypto: "What is needed is an electronic payment system based on cryptographic proof instead of trust, allowing any two willing parties to transact directly with each other without the need for a trusted third party" - Satoshi Nakamoto

Currency: "We have proposed a system for electronic transactions without relying on trust. We started with the usual framework of coins made from digital signatures, which provides strong control of ownership, but is incomplete without a way to prevent double-spending"

  • Bitcoin - A peer to peer electrontic cash system.

Bitcoin

“A purely peer-to-peer version of electronic cash”

  • Designed to generate 21 Million through “mining”
  • A global electronic cash payment system that works 24/7
  • Protected by incentive mechanisms
    • Proof-of-Work ensures a high cost to attack the system.
    • Coin Issuance ensures good actors are rewarded for their services

Bitcoin is only one type of Blockchain

A blockchain more generally tracks shared consensus.

What’s happening today with Stablecoins?

  • The GENIUS Act (Guiding and Establishing National Innovation for U.S. Stablecoins) was approved by the Senate Banking Committee March 3, 2025 with an 18-6 vote and awaits scheduling for debate with full Senate.
  • Over $233 Billion Stablecoins in circulating supply today with over $27 trillion in transfer volume in 2024.
  • It is a borderless, instantaneous, global dollar with access to "programmable money"
Source: https://www.weforum.org/stories/2025/03/stablecoins-cryptocurrency-on-rise-financial-systems/
